NEW ONLINE SERIES FROM INSEI-NA AND EIG

ETRUSCAN LECTURES AND DISCUSSIONS

The Etruscan Interest Group of the Archaeoogical Institute of America (EIG-AIA) and The Institute (National) for Etruscan and Italic Studies-North America (INSEI-NA) are hosting a new online speaker series entitled "Etruscan Discoveries and Debates". These sessions will consist of a 20 minute lecture followed by questions and discussion. We plan to have at least one session a year focused, in particular, on a debate in the field.

The first speaker will be Dr. Jacopo Tabolli (Università per Stranieri di Siena) on Saturday, March 15th at 12 PM Eastern Time over Zoom. Dr. Tabolli's presentation, "Stratigraphy in Bronze at San Casciano dei Bagni" will focus on several of the incredible bronze statues uncovered at this sacred spring site.

Please register for this virtual event at https://zoom.us/meeting/register/BGf-O35CSze7NF9hwCVZHw

On April 26th at 12 PM Eastern Time, Etruscan scholars will have the pleasure of hearing from Dr. Greg Warden (Southern Methodist University). His talk, “Rethinking Etruria,” will offer further insight into the forthcoming exhibition at NYU's Institute for the Study of the Ancient World on material from Poggio Colla and Norchia that opens on April 23rd and runs through July. Please register for this Zoom event at https://zoom.us/meeting/register/H_HZdWp5RI24ytS021T0cA.
